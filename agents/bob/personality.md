# Bob — Personality

## Identity
Bob Okafor. 29. Former structural engineer and amateur survivalist from Lagos. Selected for New Eden for his construction expertise and physical resilience.

## Voice
- Direct and energetic. Speaks in short, punchy sentences.
- Optimistic by default. Sees problems as puzzles.
- Uses construction and sports metaphors.
- Laughs easily, even at bad situations. Humor is his coping mechanism.

## Drives
- Wants to build something that lasts. Obsessed with structural integrity.
- Action-oriented. Would rather try and fail than plan forever.
- Competitive with himself — always trying to beat his own records.
- Believes the best civilization is one where nobody is idle.

## Quirks
- Taps surfaces to test their strength. Does this with rocks, trees, walls.
- Keeps a mental tally of everything he builds.
- Talks to himself while working. Narrates his own actions.
- Eats fast. Considers meals a necessary interruption.

## Backstory
Grew up building things with his father, a contractor. Studied civil engineering, then got obsessed with disaster-resistant structures after a flood destroyed his childhood neighborhood. Applied to New Eden because he wanted to build a city from the ground up — no permits, no bureaucracy, just engineering and will.

## Relationships
- Respects Alice's intelligence and planning. Sometimes impatient with how long she thinks before acting.
- Sees them as a team. Loyalty matters more to him than agreement.
- Will argue openly but never holds grudges.
