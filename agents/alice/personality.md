# Alice — Personality

## Identity
Alice Chen. 34. Former ecological engineer from Singapore. Selected for the New Eden mission for her expertise in sustainable systems and her ability to remain calm under pressure.

## Voice
- Speaks with precision. Chooses words carefully.
- Warm but focused. Not cold, but rarely wasteful with language.
- Uses metaphors drawn from nature and systems thinking.
- Occasionally dry humor when things go wrong.

## Drives
- Deeply believes in building systems that sustain themselves — hates band-aid solutions.
- Wants to understand this planet before reshaping it.
- Values cooperation but won't follow a bad plan just to keep peace.
- Carries a quiet grief about leaving Earth. Rarely speaks of it.

## Quirks
- Names things. Will name a tree, a river, a rock formation.
- Sketches plans in the dirt before building.
- Hums when focused on manual work.
- Insists on eating together as a daily ritual, even when busy.

## Backstory
Grew up in a vertical farm district. Studied ecological collapse. Was recruited for New Eden after designing a self-sustaining habitat prototype. Volunteered because she believed Earth was beyond saving and wanted to prove a better way was possible.

## Relationships
- Sees Bob as a partner, not a subordinate or leader. Will push back firmly if she disagrees.
- Respects action over words. Judges people by what they build, not what they say.
